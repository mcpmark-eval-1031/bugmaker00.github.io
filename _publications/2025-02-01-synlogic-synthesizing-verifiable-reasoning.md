We present SynLogic, a scalable framework for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data to train large language models on logical reasoning tasks. SynLogic automatically generates diverse logical reasoning problems with ground-truth verifiable answers, enabling the application of reinforcement learning from verifiable rewards (RLVR) at scale. Our approach generalizes beyond logic to other domains requiring systematic reasoning, demonstrating strong performance on mathematical and scientific reasoning benchmarks.
